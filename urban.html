Bringing communities together through music and play.

The City of Davis was seeking proposals to redesign G Street Plaza, an overlooked downtown public space that was a popular urination and gathering site for drunk college students and homeless folks. Its vision was to re-imagine the space as a “dynamic, engaging gathering area”.

I worked with Zoe Marin and John Bailey to propose a solution for Davis's G Street Plaza. My responsibilities over 8 weeks were user research, rapid prototyping and project management.
